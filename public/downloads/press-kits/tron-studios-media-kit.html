About TRON Studios

TRON Studios is the media production division of TRON DYNAMIC, specializing in creating compelling documentary films, series, and feature content that authentically represents African stories, challenges, and triumphs. Our mission is to bridge cultural gaps and showcase the rich diversity of African experiences to global audiences.

Our Vision: To become the premier destination for authentic African storytelling, creating content that educates, inspires, and drives positive change while elevating African voices on the global stage.

Featured Productions

Liberian Boy: Triumph Over War's Shadow
Documentary Series • 3 Episodes
A powerful three-part documentary series following Dr. Alvin Glay's extraordinary journey from child refugee during Liberia's civil war to becoming a successful executive in international development. The series explores themes of resilience, identity, and the power of education in transforming lives.
Runtime: 180 minutes total
Genre: Biography/Documentary
Release: Q2 2024
Languages: English, Bassa
Diaspora Voices
Anthology Series • 6 Episodes
An anthology series exploring the diverse experiences of African diaspora communities across six major cities: London, Paris, Toronto, São Paulo, Berlin, and Atlanta. Each episode tells a unique story of cultural identity, belonging, and the connections that bind the global African community.
Runtime: 45 minutes per episode
Genre: Drama/Cultural
Release: Q4 2024
Languages: Multi-language
Rising Tides
Feature Film • 120 Minutes
A compelling feature film about three young entrepreneurs in Lagos who navigate the challenges of building a tech startup while addressing gender inequality and social justice issues in Nigeria's rapidly evolving business landscape.
Runtime: 120 minutes
Genre: Drama/Thriller
Release: Q1 2025
Languages: English, Yoruba
